Who We Are

Ting is an Air Force optometrist addicted to rock climbing. Having moved all over the U.S., she found the old-school bulletin board system frustratingly inefficient. For one, she cannot see the contact list until she is at the gym, then she has to individually type/dial each contact, and lastly, she never gets an instant reply. That is how the idea of creating an online hub of climbers was born. She envisions a centralized climbing network where anyone can find climbers ready to climb in real time. She also hopes to occasionally score a free guest pass when visiting a new gym with a member;) Ting thinks she is pretty clever in scoring the name belayloop. Anyone else love a wordplay?

Donovan is a computer geek, entrepreneur, and rock climbing enthusiast. After running a successful bitcoin trading business for three years, he continued to pursue his passion for technology by working as a freelance web developer. Donovan spends much of his time writing code and rock climbing. He is a passionate JavaScript developer and enjoys creating full stack web applications using React, Redux, and Express. He loves learning new technologies and is experienced with the MERN stack (Mongo, Express, React, and Node).

Belayloop: Made by Climbers for Climbers

Belayloop envisions a future where new climbers no longer have to rely on Bulletin Boards, Reddit, Facebook, or Tinder for finding a belayer; a future where all climbers can access Belayloop's web hub to find each other. If you would like to contribute to this project, please reach out to us at connect@belayloop.com. We welcome all suggestions, tips, critiques, and anything else you wish to share with us:) Thank you for your interest and hope to see you on the wall!
